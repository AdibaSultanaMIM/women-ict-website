About WE-ICT

Organized by the Department of CSE, BUET, this workshop aims to encourage, support, and connect Bangladeshi women in computing through higher studies and research.

Our Vision

To bridge the digital divide and empower women to lead in the global tech ecosystem.

Academic Focus

Providing a forum for researchers and industry professionals to exchange success stories (from flyer).
